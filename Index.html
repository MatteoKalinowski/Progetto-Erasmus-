About Us

30 Years of Experience

Founded in 1993 in the city of Coimbra, ARL – Assistência, Redes e Computação, Lda (VAT / Tax ID: 502990031) provides information and communication technology services, standing out as a center of excellence in technical support. With over 30 years of knowledge and experience, you can fully trust us to resolve any issues with your equipment.

Our assistance and repair services cover all IT equipment. We provide support both at our walk-in local store in Coimbra and directly at our clients' premises. We offer quick repairs for the general public, as well as specialized services for small, medium, and large corporations, both nationwide and internationally.

Our wide range of services includes equipment installation, repair, and maintenance, network design and management, and website development. Crucially, all repairs are manufacturer-certified. For your convenience, your devices can be picked up from your home via courier services, adhering to all necessary security procedures, and delivered back directly to your preferred location.
